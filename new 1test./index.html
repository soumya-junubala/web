Make Your Own Panipuri Adventure

Addendum

Your Panipuri Adventure:

  • Embark on a journey through the vibrant world of panipuri.
  • Make choices to visit stalls, try different flavors, or create your own panipuri at home.
  • Share your panipuri experience and reflect on your delightful adventure.
Challenges Faced:
  • Dynamic display of images based on user choices.
  • Ensuring a seamless and engaging narrative flow for a delightful experience.
Creative Choices:
  • Randomizing background images to provide a variety of experiences.
  • Using full-screen images to emphasize key moments in the panipuri adventure.
Insights Gained:
  • Importance of user immersion in interactive storytelling.
  • Balancing narrative depth with user agency for an enjoyable experience.
